Hussein Tayrani

Motion Graphics

Hussein is currently a professor at Algonquin College in the School of Media and Design. He teaches Design, Video and Motion Graphics courses for IMD. Helping his students to thrive and become professionals at work are among his main goals. Hussein's drive is powered by a special fuel he identifies as his 'Coffeetivity', the combination of coffee with creativity. When his colleagues see him tackling a project with a cup of coffee in his hand, there is no doubt he is up to something genius.

Susan Murphy

Project Management

Over the years I’ve produced many shows on a variety of topics – music, arts, multicultural, documentaries, reality TV and yes, even politics! also work part time at Algonquin College as a program coordinator and curriculum developer in the School of Continuing and Online Learning.
