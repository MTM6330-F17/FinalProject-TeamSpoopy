Program Overview

Algonquin College

Interactive Media Design

This 2 year Ontario College Diploma program is designed to prepare students for a variety of careers in digital media, with equal emphasis on creative and technical aspects.

Start Date: Sept. 4, 2018

Learn More

Program

There is strong emphasis placed on media production in courses like Web Development, Digital Imaging, Animation, Photography and Videography. Students execute team media projects to gain practical experience with live clients.

Success Factors

This program is suitable for highly adaptable students with current technology, those who have a vast imaginiation, that enjoy problem solving, and students who also enjoy a hands on approach to learning about multimedia

Learning Outcomes

1. Complete both individual and team interactive projects effectively.
2. Use best practices to design and develop media content.
3. Contribute to the development, planning and professional presentation of an interactive media project.
4. Build effective Web sites and/or mobile applications.
5. Apply research and conceptual skills to propose solutions for mobile/multimedia/Web development problems.
6. Use creative and critical thinking techniques in the effective design, development and implementation of a media project.
Jan 10, 2018 By Program Administrator

Program Eligibility

English, Grade 12 (ENG4C or equivalent).
Applicants with International transcripts must provide proof of the subject specific requirements noted above along with proof of either: IELTS-International English Language Testing Service-Overall band of 6.0 with a minimum of 5.5 in each band; OR TOEFL-Internet-based (iBT)-overall 80, with the minimum of 20 in each component: Reading: 20; Listening: 20; Speaking: 20; Writing: 20.
