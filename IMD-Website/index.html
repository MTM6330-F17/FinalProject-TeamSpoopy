Welcome to IMD

About the Program

Interactive Media Design is a two-year program offered at Algonquin College. It teaches students a variety of different skills in today's constantly evolving technology industry. Film, design and website production are the program's main focuses.

Learn More

Why Choose IMD

IMD has tons to offer to students interested in video, graphic design, and website production.

Intro to DSLR

Learn photography basics with DSLR cameras in the first semester. This will be a stepping stone into professional photography and an entry into the film industry.

Digital Graphics

Students will gain skills in both Photoshop and Illustrator which are two key tools involved in any design work you will do throughout the program and will carry over into your career.

Video Foundations

Learn basic video fundamentals like rule of thirds, different types of shots, storyboarding, and audio techniques for recording interviews. The course will go over a variety of equipment you may use throughout your career.

Web Development

Via a multitude of different courses, students will have the opportunity to learn how to do both front-end and back-end web development. You will learn a variety of coding languages such as: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Sass, etc.

UX Design

Go through the process of user-experience design. This course will teach you how to identify a user's needs, gather information required to properly map out a website, create website mockups, and then create prototypes which you can use to run user-testing.

Applied Projects

This course is a key component to the program as it allows students to gain an understanding of what it's like to work with clients. This course also prepares students for co-op if they choose to do so.
